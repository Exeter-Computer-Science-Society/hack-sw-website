Hack SouthWest

A Hackathon for university students from Exeter, Bournemouth, Bristol,
Bath and Plymouth.

Feb 3rd, 12:00 AM

University of Exeter, South West Institute of Technology

ABOUT

Organised by the computer science society of University Of Exeter, Hack South West is the most fun and enjoyable experience for students around the uk. Available to everyone from beginners to experts, this is a great opportunity to get into hackathons. Our welcoming atmosphere will surely encourage you to go to more!


We plan on running a 24 hour event which will begin with registration and proceed to be ran overnight. Food and drinks will be provided throughout the duration of the event so theres no need to worry about that. We will also be providing a variety of workshops and talks to help you get started with your hackathon project.


We will be providing a variety of prizes for the best hacks. These will be announced closer to the event.

Timeline

  • 12:00

    registration begins at 12:00 sharp. Make your way to the registration stand to tick off your name on the list.

  • 13:45

    We will have a welcome talk at 13:45 to introduce the event and explain the rules. Additionally there will be a talk from our sponsors and external speakers to help you get started with the hackathon.

  • 14:00

    Begin of the Hack-Sw at 14:00. You will have 24 hours to complete the project. Thoughout the hackathon we will run minigames and provide food free of charge to all participants.

  • 14:00 -> 14:00

    For the duration of the hackathon we will be running a variety of activities to keep you entertained. These will include minigames, workshops, movies. Some of the activities we have planned are

    • Nerf Battle
    • Cup Stacking Competition
    • Card Stacking Competition
    • Information Stands
    • Movies
    • Among Us Lobbies
    • Typing Speed Contest

  • 14:00

    The award ceremony will take place at 14:00. We will announce the winners of the hackathon and provide prizes for the fastest time of completion of the task. Rewards will be provided by our sponsors.
